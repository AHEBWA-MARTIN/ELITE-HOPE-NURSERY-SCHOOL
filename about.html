About us

Message From Directors

Elite Hope Nursery School is a private mixed, day and boarding school. It is located in Nyamiko cell, Kihunda Ward, kagango Division, in Sheema municipality, about 6miles along kabwohe-kihunda-Nyihanga Road. The school was started in May 2021 in a family household room, It started with only 11 baby class learners, 3 middle class learners and 2 top class learners, in a free Left house room building that contained the office, classroom and a store. The school was founded by a group of highly motivated members led by Mr. AHEBWA MARTIN as their chairman.

The others were Miss Nampebwa Zariika, Miss. Kabamwere Molly, Miss Kampasika Ritah who still currently act as School Teachers as Shown in Images on the right hand side of this current page

The starting was not easy as one would expect, However their determination to establish a model, regional school coupled with immense support from parents ,enabled members to carry on with their dream. all the classes from Baby Class - Top Class have been established. The pioneer and the current head teacher, Miss. Nampebwa Zariika did a great job in ensuring that the young institution kept on flourishing with good administration and prudent leadership from the Board of Directors coupled with hardworking staff, Elite Hope Nursery school has over the years grown into this mighty institution, with not all that magnificent structures an enrolment of Over 90 chldren and over 4 employees.

The school is developing steadily and is destined to be acentre of academic excellence.
